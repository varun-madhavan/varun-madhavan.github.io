## Project Overview
This project built demand forecasts for first-mile pickup planning and routing at multiple geographic and temporal resolutions.

## What We Built
- Developed gradient-boosting forecasting pipelines for package-volume prediction.
- Incorporated scalable batch inference for high-throughput deployment.
- Supported cost-sensitive planning for route and pickup operations.

## Why It Matters
Better demand forecasts improve resource allocation and reduce operational costs in pickup logistics.
