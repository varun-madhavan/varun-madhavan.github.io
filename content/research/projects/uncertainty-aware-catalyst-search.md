## Project Overview
This project focused on reliable machine-learning-guided catalyst screening for adsorption energy prediction on alloy systems. The main question was not only *which predictions are accurate*, but also *which predictions are trustworthy enough* to prioritize for expensive follow-up analysis.

## What We Built
- Evaluated uncertainty quantification strategies on OC20 IS2RE adsorption-energy prediction workflows.
- Compared methods on accuracy, calibration, and dispersion quality.
- Investigated evidential regression as a practical alternative to MC dropout and k-fold ensembling.

## Why It Matters
Uncertainty-aware ranking can reduce wasted experimental or simulation cycles by prioritizing candidates with favorable performance-confidence tradeoffs.
