## Project Overview
This work introduced package-level unsupervised pre-training to improve downstream logistics prediction tasks.

## What We Built
- Designed a next-state prediction objective for sequential package events.
- Integrated variable-length route plans, static metadata, and scan history into shared representations.
- Transferred learned representations to tasks such as delay prediction, loss detection, and shipping cost estimation.

## Why It Matters
Shared pre-trained representations can provide a scalable way to improve multiple operational models simultaneously.
