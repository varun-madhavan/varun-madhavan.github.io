## Project Overview
This project developed a machine-learning surrogate for computationally expensive pandemic agent-based simulators used for evaluating intervention policies.

## What We Built
- Trained surrogate models to emulate intervention-outcome mappings from ABM outputs.
- Reduced evaluation time for counterfactual policy scenarios while controlling predictive error.
- Demonstrated practical speedup for policy analyses involving masking, tracing, and vaccination scenarios.

## Why It Matters
Fast but reliable surrogate models enable broader scenario exploration and more timely decision support.
