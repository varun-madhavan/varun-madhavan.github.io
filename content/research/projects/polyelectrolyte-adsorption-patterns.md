## Project Overview
This thesis investigated adsorption behavior of polyelectrolytes on patterned charged surfaces using molecular dynamics simulations.

## What We Built
- Simulated adsorption under variations in concentration, bond stiffness, pattern size, and charge density.
- Characterized how surface pattern periodicity changes adsorption extent and morphology.
- Documented mechanistic trends relevant to pattern-guided molecular design.

## Why It Matters
Understanding adsorption response to patterning can inform design of functional interfaces and materials.
