## Project Overview
This competition project produced a domain-specific QA system combining retrieval and generation components under strict CPU-only constraints.

## What We Built
- Built a retrieval-augmented question-answering pipeline over arbitrary corpora.
- Optimized the retriever/reranker stack for low-latency CPU inference.
- Achieved strong accuracy-efficiency tradeoffs for constrained deployment settings.

## Why It Matters
Practical QA systems often operate under compute constraints, so robust low-resource design is critical.
