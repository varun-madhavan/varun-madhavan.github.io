## Project Overview
RCTransformer was designed to estimate last-mile shipping costs from package route-plan information and rate-card constraints.

## What We Built
- Created a transformer architecture tailored to structured route-plan sequences.
- Benchmarked against tabular baselines including FT-Transformer and TabTransformer.
- Improved estimation quality for downstream planning and operational cost analysis.

## Why It Matters
Better shipping-cost estimates improve planning reliability and can directly influence logistics efficiency.
