## Project Overview
This internship project aimed to reduce post-call administrative burden for customer-support agents through automated NLP pipelines.

## What We Built
- Fine-tuned language models for call summarization and issue extraction.
- Used GPT-4-annotated instruction data to supervise model behavior.
- Applied training optimizations such as flash attention and zero redundancy optimizers for faster iteration.

## Why It Matters
Reducing wrap time improves agent productivity and accelerates customer issue resolution.
