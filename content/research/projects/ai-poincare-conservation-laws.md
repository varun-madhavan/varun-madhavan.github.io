## Project Overview
AI Poincare targeted automatic discovery of conservation laws directly from governing differential equations. The objective was to recover interpretable conserved quantities without relying on ergodic trajectory assumptions.

## What We Built
- A numerical pipeline that parameterizes candidate invariants with neural models.
- A symbolic discovery pipeline with brute-force search and fast rejection heuristics.
- Validation on benchmark systems such as the three-body problem, KdV, and nonlinear Schrodinger dynamics.

## Why It Matters
Automating conservation-law discovery supports interpretable scientific AI and can accelerate equation understanding in unfamiliar systems.
